Harbor Seal

The Harbor Seal, known as the common seal, is found along temperate anr Arctic coastlines of the Northern Hemisphere.

  • Scientific Name: Phoca vitulina
  • Average Length: 1.85 meters
  • Average Lifespan: 20-35 years
  • Habitat: Coasts and beaches

Harbor seals are solitary but are gregarious when hauled out and during the breeding season. Individual harbor seals possess a unique pattern of spots, either dark on a light background or light on a dark.
